ABOUT ME

I'm a

MATHEMATICS GRADUATE

I graduated from Newcastle University in 2021 with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics, where I developed strong analytical and problem-solving skills. My studies emphasized data analysis, statistical methods, mathematical modeling, and programming, equipping me with tools to tackle complex challenges in a variety of fields.

One of my key projects involved programming a digital version of the Monopoly board game, where I applied probability and strategic modeling to recreate the household game.
